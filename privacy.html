Overview

TAN Creative (“we”, “us”) is an Istanbul-based interdisciplinary design studio. This page explains what information we collect when you visit tancreativestudio.com or get in touch with us, and how we use it. We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the site and respond to enquiries.

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or submit personal data to browse this site. If you email us at info@tancreativestudio.com, we receive the contact details and message content you choose to share, solely to reply to you.

Our hosting provider may keep standard server logs (such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested) for security and performance purposes.

How We Use It

We use the information above only to respond to your enquiries, to maintain and improve the website, and to keep it secure. We do not sell your personal data.

Cookies & Analytics

This site aims to run without non-essential cookies. If analytics or embedded third-party content is added in the future, this section will be updated to describe it and your choices.

Third Parties

We rely on a hosting provider to serve this website and on standard email services to receive your messages. These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data you have shared with us. To make a request, email info@tancreativestudio.com and we will respond within a reasonable time frame.
